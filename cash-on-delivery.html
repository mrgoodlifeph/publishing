Cash on Delivery

Convenient payment option for your book orders

How Does It Work?

  1. COD payments are available for orders not exceeding a total amount of ₱15,000.
  2. Place your order at our online shop and upon checkout, you will be prompted to select a payment method. Choose Cash on Delivery (COD).
  3. Shipping fees apply based on your location. Standard shipping rates are ₱150 for Metro Manila and ₱200 for provincial destinations.
  4. COD orders will be delivered via our trusted courier partners. Delivery time varies by location.

Order Cancellation

Your order may be cancelled if it has not yet entered the shipping stage. Please contact us via email at mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com or call us at +639554659229 for assistance.

Can a Customer Refuse to Pay and Receive a COD Order?

  1. The only valid reason for refusing to receive and/or pay for a COD order is incorrect information (e.g., a different payment amount than what was confirmed via email, or wrong items delivered). Please inform the delivery person immediately so we can promptly address the issue.
  2. Refusing a COD order for any other reason, such as false claims of incorrect products delivered, constitutes a serious violation of our COD service terms. A mere change of mind will not be accepted. We reserve the right to refuse service to customers who violate the terms of any of our services.

When Will My Order Be Delivered and How Will I Be Notified?

  1. After you have placed your order, it will be sent to our facility for processing. Once orders are dispatched, we will send you an email with your tracking details. Our logistics partner will contact you by phone or SMS on the day of delivery. Please respond as soon as possible to facilitate immediate delivery.
  2. In the event that the parcel is undeliverable (where the courier has made reasonable attempts to deliver the parcel, but it is either refused or no delivery can be made), the account will be evaluated and may be barred from placing further COD orders.
  3. We are continuously improving our services, and we hope this payment option will enhance your online buying experience with us.

Important Notes

  • Please ensure that someone is available to receive the order at the delivery address.
  • Have the exact amount ready to expedite the delivery process.
  • Inspect your order upon receipt and report any issues immediately to the delivery personnel.
  • Keep your order confirmation email for reference.
