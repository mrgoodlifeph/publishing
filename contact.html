Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the publishing process take?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope and complexity. A typical book project takes 2-6 months from manuscript to finished product. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer rush services?

Yes, we can accommodate urgent projects. Rush service fees may apply depending on the timeline and requirements. Contact us to discuss your deadline.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept most common formats including Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx), PDF, InDesign, and plain text files. Contact us if you have files in other formats.

Do you provide samples of your work?

Absolutely! We'd be happy to show you samples of our previous projects during a consultation. This helps you understand our quality and capabilities.

Can I visit your office?

Yes! We welcome office visits during business hours. We recommend calling ahead to ensure someone is available to give you proper attention and answer your questions.
