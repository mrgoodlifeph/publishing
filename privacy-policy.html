Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Greetings, and thank you for visiting the MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING website. At MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is a priority. We have crafted this Privacy Policy that outlines the methods of collection, utilization, disclosure, sharing, and storage of your personal information provided via our website and services.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy carefully and reach out to us with any inquiries you may have.

Types of Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide to us which is reasonably necessary for our business functions and activities.

Personal Information We Collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Home address/Office address
  • Contact number(s)
  • Details of your interactions with our customer support team
  • Order history and preferences

Information Collected Indirectly:

Although we generally collect personal information directly from you, we may also collect certain types of personal information about you indirectly through our third-party providers such as:

  • Analytics Information: This includes data from analytics tools which aid us in analyzing website traffic and improving user experience.
  • Financial/Transaction Details: Provided by our payment processors to facilitate transactions.

The scope of personal data collected by these third-party services is determined by their respective privacy settings and policies.

How We Use, Secure, & Store Your Personal Information

We employ widely recognized technical and organizational safeguards to shield your personal data from accidental or illegal destruction, loss, alteration, or damage. The personal data we gather is housed on protected servers. Furthermore, all electronic transactions conducted via our website are secured using encryption technology, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of your information.

We May Process Your Personal Information For:

  • Ensuring the availability of our products and services to you, from the point of order to delivery.
  • Supporting our internal operations, including but not limited to auditing, data analytics, and research, which help enhance our offerings, services, and customer communication.
  • Facilitating seamless communication between you and our representatives.
  • Adhering to legal and regulatory obligations, as well as to assist in the identification and prevention of theft, fraud, and other criminal activities.
  • For any additional purposes, provided you have granted explicit permission for such use.

Third-Party Service Providers:

We partner with reputable third-party service providers for the following purposes:

  • To complete transactions, including payment processing and delivery of orders.
  • To safeguard against fraud by protecting your financial details from misuse.

Rest assured, your email address will remain confidential. We pledge not to share, sell, lease, exchange, or permit third-party providers to utilize it for commercial aims without receiving your explicit approval.

Log Data

Please be aware that each time you access our website, we gather data transmitted by your browser, known as Log Data. This may encompass details like your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, the type of browser you use, the specific pages of our website you explore, the date and time of your visit, the duration of your stay on those pages, along with various other statistical data.

Service Providers

We engage with external companies and professionals for several key reasons:

  • To enhance the functionality of our services
  • To manage the services on our behalf
  • To execute services related to our business
  • To help us understand the usage patterns of our services

Please be advised that in carrying out these duties, these third parties may have access to your Personal Information. This access is strictly for the purpose of fulfilling their assigned responsibilities for us. They are, however, bound by a duty of confidentiality and are not permitted to use or reveal your information for any unrelated activities.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device that help us understand how you use our site and remember your preferences. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality of our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on consent to process your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may undergo occasional revisions. We recommend that you regularly check this page for updates. Any amendments will be communicated by publishing the revised Privacy Policy on this page. Please note that these updates take effect immediately upon being posted on this page.
