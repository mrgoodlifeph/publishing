Return Policy

Information about returns, refunds, and exchanges

Return Policy

At MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every purchase. This Return Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returning products purchased from our store.

Defective Book Returns

  1. Replacements for Defective Books: MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING offers replacements for books with defects such as missing pages, printing errors, damaged bindings, or other manufacturing defects. We cover all shipping and handling fees for such returns.
  2. Return Notification: Should you need to return a defective book, please inform us within three (3) working days of receiving it. Include the reason or the specific defect, and contact us at mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com or call us at +639554659229. Upon approval, we will issue a replacement notice.
  3. Refund Processing: Once we receive the returned book and verify the defect, we will initiate the refund process within three (3) working days.
  4. Incorrect Orders: Returns or replacements due to customer error, or a change of mind, are not supported by MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING. Please ensure you review your order carefully before completing your purchase.

Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order provided that the order has not yet undergone our shipping process. To request a cancellation, please send an email to mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com or call us at +639554659229 as soon as possible. Include your order number and reason for cancellation.

Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled but may be eligible for return upon receipt according to our return policy.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, send us an email at mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com and send your item to:

MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING
Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, 3500

Please ensure the item is in its original condition with all packaging materials included. Include a copy of your order confirmation or receipt.

Refund Process

Refunds will be processed according to the original payment method:

  • Cash on Delivery: Refunds will be issued via bank transfer or GCash. Please provide your account details.
  • Online Payment: Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method within 7-14 business days.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund yet, please follow these steps:

  1. Recheck Your Bank Account: Initially, revisit your bank account to ensure the refund hasn't been processed.
  2. Credit Card Company Inquiry: Subsequently, reach out to your credit card issuer, as refunds can sometimes take a while to be reflected.
  3. Bank Confirmation: Following that, get in touch with your bank, as there's typically a processing interval for refunds to be recorded.
  4. Further Assistance: If you've followed these steps and the refund is still pending, please contact us directly at mrgoodlifeph@gmail.com for support.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Books showing signs of use, wear, or damage not caused by defects
  • Items purchased during clearance sales (unless defective)
  • Digital products or downloadable content
  • Personalized or custom-printed materials

Return Shipping

For defective or incorrect items, MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING will cover the return shipping costs. We will provide you with a prepaid shipping label or reimburse your shipping expenses upon approval of your return request.

For all other returns (if approved on a case-by-case basis), the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.
