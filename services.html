Getting Started with POD

Follow these simple steps to publish your book with our Print-on-Demand service.

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Submit a complete print-out or PDF of your manuscript with detailed formatting instructions. The file must be complete and edited.

2

Submit Graphics & Images

Provide all graphics, illustrations, and pictures. We can scan up to 10 images as part of the package.

3

Choose Your Package

Select from our Classic, Journal, or Custom packages based on your needs and budget.

4

Review Proof Copies

Receive 2 proof copies for review. We allocate 2 hours for basic corrections. Additional changes may incur extra charges.

5

Approve for Production

Once you approve the proofs, we proceed to production. Your book can be ready within 10 working days!

6

Launch & Distribution

Receive your books and optionally join our Books on Demand distribution program.
