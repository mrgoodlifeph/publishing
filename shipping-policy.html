Shipping Policy

Information about delivery and shipping of your orders

Delivery/Shipping Policies

  1. Orders are processed daily from Monday to Saturday. Orders received before 12:00 PM (Philippine time) will be processed on the same business day, while orders received after 12:00 PM are processed on the next business day.
  2. Sold products are packed and available for courier pickup within twenty-four (24) hours after the order has been processed and approved.
  3. If an order will not be available within 24 hours or is out-of-stock, the customer will be notified via email and will be advised if and when the item will become available.
  4. The customer has the option to have the order cancelled, and the customer will not be charged for the cancelled order.
  5. To track the status of your order, please check your email for tracking information or contact us directly.

Shipping

  1. Orders are normally dispatched on the next business day following the day the order was confirmed to the customer. Estimated delivery within Metro Manila will be 2-3 business days, provided the item is available in our facility. Orders to be delivered to provincial destinations will take 5 to 7 business days from the process date. A tracking number from the courier will be provided.
  2. MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING does not assume responsibility for and shall not be liable for any damages, whether direct, incidental, special, or consequential, arising from the handling and delivery of order goods by the authorized couriers. However, MR-GOODLIFE PUBLISHING shall properly coordinate with its authorized courier who is responsible for the shipment to properly compensate the customer.

Courier Transit Time

  • Within Tuguegarao City: 1-2 working days
  • Within Cagayan Valley Region: 2-3 working days
  • Metro Manila: 2-3 working days
  • Provincial Areas: 5-7 working days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 working days

Note: Transit times may vary depending on weather conditions, courier availability, and other unforeseen circumstances.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with your tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor your shipment's progress. If you have any questions about your order status, please contact our customer service team.

Delivery Instructions

  • Please ensure that someone is available at the delivery address to receive your order.
  • If no one is available, the courier will attempt delivery on the next business day or contact you to arrange redelivery.
  • For security reasons, we may require a valid ID upon delivery for high-value orders.
  • Please inspect your package upon receipt and report any damages immediately.
